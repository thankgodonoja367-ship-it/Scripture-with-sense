📖 Scripture 1

James 1:22 — “Do not merely listen to the word… do what it says.”

It’s easy to hear God’s word, agree with it, and still not change anything.

This verse exposes that. Listening without action is self-deception. You feel like you’re growing, but nothing in your life is actually changing.

Growth is not what you know. It’s what you do consistently.

📖 Scripture 2

Proverbs 4:23 — “Guard your heart, for everything you do flows from it.”

Your life doesn’t just happen. It flows from what you allow into your heart.

The content you consume, the people you listen to, the thoughts you entertain… they are shaping your direction whether you notice it or not.

If you don’t guard it, you will live a life you didn’t intend.

🤔 Real Question

“Why do I keep struggling with the same sin even though I pray?”

Because prayer without action changes nothing.

You’re asking for help, but not changing the habits or environments feeding the problem.

🤔 Real Question

“How do I stay consistent with God?”

Stop depending on motivation.

Build simple daily habits. Even when you don’t feel like it. Consistency is built, not felt.
